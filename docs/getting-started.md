# Getting Started with DreamTeam

Welcome to DreamTeam, your all-in-one platform for business management, AI automation, and financial clarity.

## What is DreamTeam?

DreamTeam combines everything you need to run your business in one connected platform:

- **Complete financial visibility** - Connect all your bank accounts and see your full financial picture
- **Sales and CRM** - Track leads, manage deals, and grow your pipeline
- **Team communication** - Channels, direct messages, and video huddles
- **Project management** - Tasks, milestones, and progress tracking
- **Knowledge base** - Documents, whiteboards, and searchable company knowledge
- **AI agents** - Intelligent assistants that help automate your work

---

## Quick Start (Your First 5 Minutes)

### 1. Sign Up

1. Visit [app.dreamteam.com](https://app.dreamteam.com) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Check your email for a verification link
4. Click the link to verify your account

### 2. Initial Setup

After signing in, you'll land on your **Finance Dashboard**. This is your home base for financial visibility.

### 3. Connect Your First Bank Account

1. Click **Accounts** in the sidebar
2. Click **Connect Bank**
3. Search for your bank in the Plaid connection window
4. Log in with your bank credentials (handled securely by Plaid)
5. Select which accounts to link
6. Your transactions will begin syncing automatically

### 4. Navigate the Dashboard

Use the **workspace switcher** in the top-left to move between the six main areas:

| Workspace | Description | Keyboard Shortcut |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Finance | Financial management and analytics | `⌘1` |
| Sales | CRM, leads, and pipeline | `⌘2` |
| Team | Messaging and communication | `⌘3` |
| Projects | Task and project tracking | `⌘4` |
| Knowledge | Documents and whiteboards | `⌘5` |
| Agents | AI assistants | — |

## Workspace Guides

### Finance

The Finance workspace gives you complete visibility into your money.

**Dashboard Overview**
- **Net Worth** - Total assets minus liabilities across all connected accounts
- **Income & Expenses** - Monthly cash flow at a glance
- **Account Balances** - Real-time balances from all connected banks

**Key Features**

| Feature | How to Access | What It Does |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Accounts | Sidebar → Accounts | View all connected accounts and balances |
| Transactions | Sidebar → Transactions | Browse, search, and categorize all transactions |
| Budgets | Sidebar → Budgets | Set spending limits by category and track progress |
| Analytics | Sidebar → Analytics | Deep-dive charts: cash flow, expenses, income, P&L |
| Goals | Sidebar → Goals | Set financial targets (revenue, profit, exit planning) |

**Getting Started with Finance**
1. Connect at least one bank account
2. Review your transactions and adjust any miscategorized items
3. Create your first budget by clicking **Budgets → New Budget**
4. Explore **Analytics** to understand your spending patterns

---

### Sales

The Sales workspace is your CRM for managing customer relationships and closing deals.

**CRM Overview**
- **Pipeline** - Visual kanban board of all deals by stage
- **Leads** - Potential customers to qualify
- **Contacts** - Your full contact database
- **Deals** - Active opportunities you're working

**Key Features**

| Feature | How to Access | What It Does |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Leads | Sidebar → Leads | Add and qualify potential customers |
| Contacts | Sidebar → Contacts | Manage your contact database |
| Deals | Sidebar → Deals | Track opportunities with value and stage |
| Pipeline | Sidebar → Pipeline | Drag-and-drop deals through stages |
| Activities | Sidebar → Activities | Log calls, emails, meetings |
| Reports | Sidebar → Reports | Sales performance and funnel analytics |

**Getting Started with Sales**
1. Add your first lead by clicking **Leads → New Lead**
2. When a lead is qualified, convert them to a **Contact** and create a **Deal**
3. Move deals through your pipeline stages as they progress
4. Log activities to keep a complete history of interactions

### Team

The Team workspace is your communication hub for real-time collaboration.

**Communication Overview**
- **Channels** - Shared spaces for team discussions by topic
- **Direct Messages** - Private 1:1 or small group conversations
- **Huddles** - Quick audio/video calls right from chat

**Key Features**

| Feature | How to Access | What It Does |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Channels | Sidebar → Channels | Browse and join team channels |
| Direct Messages | Sidebar → DMs | Message individuals or small groups |
| Mentions | Sidebar → Mentions | See all messages where you're @mentioned |
| Huddles | Click huddle icon in any channel | Start instant voice/video calls |
| Search | ⌘K or sidebar → Search | Find messages across all conversations |

**Getting Started with Team**
1. Browse existing channels or create your first channel
2. Send a message to introduce yourself
3. Use **@mentions** to get someone's attention
4. Start a huddle when you need a quick face-to-face conversation

### Projects

The Projects workspace helps you manage work and track progress.

**Project Overview**
- **Projects** - Containers for related tasks and milestones
- **Tasks** - Individual work items with assignees and due dates
- **Milestones** - Key checkpoints and deliverables

**Key Features**

| Feature | How to Access | What It Does |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Projects | Sidebar → Projects | View all projects and their status |
| My Tasks | Sidebar → My Tasks | See tasks assigned to you across all projects |
| Timeline | Sidebar → Timeline | Gantt-style view of project schedules |
| Milestones | Sidebar → Milestones | Track key deliverables and deadlines |
| Workload | Sidebar → Workload | See team capacity and assignments |

**Getting Started with Projects**
1. Create your first project by clicking **Projects → New Project**
2. Add tasks to the project with descriptions and due dates
3. Assign tasks to team members
4. Set milestones for major deliverables
5. Track progress using the project board or timeline view

### Knowledge

The Knowledge workspace is your team's shared brain for documents and ideas.

**Knowledge Overview**
- **Pages** - Rich documents for notes, wikis, and documentation
- **Whiteboards** - Visual canvases for brainstorming and diagrams
- **Search** - Find anything across your knowledge base instantly

**Key Features**

| Feature | How to Access | What It Does |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Pages | Sidebar → Pages | Create and edit documents |
| All Pages | Sidebar → All | Browse your complete page library |
| Whiteboards | Sidebar → Whiteboards | Create visual boards and diagrams |
| Search | ⌘K or sidebar → Search | Find content across all pages |

**Getting Started with Knowledge**
1. Create your first page by clicking **New Page**
2. Use the editor to add text, headings, lists, and embedded content
3. Organize pages with nested hierarchies
4. Create a whiteboard for visual brainstorming
5. Use search to quickly find any content

### Agents

The Agents workspace is where you meet your AI teammates.

**Agents Overview**
- **Discover** - Browse available AI agents with different specialties
- **My Agents** - Agents you've hired and activated
- **Activity** - See what your agents are working on
- **Chat** - Interact with agents through conversation

**Key Features**

| Feature | How to Access | What It Does |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Discover | Agents → Discover | Browse the agent directory |
| My Agents | Agents → My Agents | View hired/active agents |
| Activity | Agents → Activity | Monitor agent actions and logs |
| Chat | Click on any agent | Start a conversation |
| Pending | Activity → Pending | Review actions awaiting your approval |

**Getting Started with Agents**
1. Visit **Discover** to browse available AI agents
2. Click on an agent to learn about their capabilities
3. **Hire** an agent to add them to your team
4. Open a chat with the agent to start interacting
5. Watch the **reasoning panel** to see how the agent thinks
6. Review the **tool usage** to see what actions the agent takes

**Understanding Agent Interactions**
- **Reasoning** - Agents show their thought process as they work
- **Tool Usage** - Agents can use tools to access data, send messages, or take actions
- **Approvals** - Some actions require your confirmation before executing
- **Scheduling** - Set agents to run tasks on a schedule

## Mobile App Guide

DreamTeam is fully available on iOS and Android.

### Downloading the App

1. Open the **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play** (Android)
2. Search for "DreamTeam"
3. Download and install the app

### Logging In

1. Open the DreamTeam app
2. Tap **Log In**
3. Enter your email and password
4. You're in!

### Mobile Features

The mobile app includes all six workspaces:

- **Finance** - Check balances, view transactions, manage budgets on the go
- **Sales** - Access your CRM, add leads, and update deals from anywhere
- **Team** - Send messages, join channels, and start huddles
- **Projects** - View and update tasks from your phone
- **Knowledge** - Access and create pages and notes
- **Agents** - Chat with AI agents anytime

### Mobile Tips

- **Pull to refresh** - Swipe down on any list to get the latest data
- **Quick actions** - Long-press items for contextual actions
- **Notifications** - Enable push notifications to stay on top of mentions and updates
- **Offline access** - Core data is cached for offline viewing
- **Bottom navigation** - Tap the tabs at the bottom to switch workspaces

## Tips & Shortcuts

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `⌘1` | Switch to Finance |
| `⌘2` | Switch to Sales |
| `⌘3` | Switch to Team |
| `⌘4` | Switch to Projects |
| `⌘5` | Switch to Knowledge |
| `⌘K` | Open quick search / command palette |
| `⌘N` | Create new item (context-aware) |
| `⌘/` | Show all keyboard shortcuts |

### Quick Actions

- **Command Palette** - Press `⌘K` to search anything: pages, contacts, transactions, or run quick actions
- **New Item** - Press `⌘N` to create a new item in your current context
- **Quick Switch** - Use `⌘1-5` to instantly jump between workspaces
